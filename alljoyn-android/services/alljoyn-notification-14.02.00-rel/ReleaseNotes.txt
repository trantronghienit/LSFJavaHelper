Features added in Version 14.02
-------------------------------

The Notification Service provides manufacturer’s of connected-devices with a standardized interface for sending simple, human-readable, Notifications from headless “AllJoyn Producer” devices to their more intelligent “AllJoyn Consumer” devices, for display to the End-User.
The notification service enables Alljoyn producer devices to send notifications to AJ consumer devices. 
The notification service is provided as an AJ core service for use across multiple application developers.

This was tested with 5 Producers and 5 Consumers.
